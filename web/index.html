Free your notes

Leave expensive note-taking apps without losing your workflow.

EverFree turns your notes into Markdown, syncs them to your own private GitHub repository, and keeps them usable from web, mobile, and the native Mac app.

Use the Mac app for the first Evernote migration. After your notes are backed up, this web app and the mobile web app read and write the same GitHub-backed notes.
