                    Product thesis
                    
Not a Markdown pitch. Not a prettier notes database.

                    

                        EverFree is for the moment after capture: when you have notes, scraps, research, and an unfinished draft, and you need a writing partner inside the editor.
                    

                

                

                    

                        write
                        Continue a draft, rewrite selected text, summarize a messy note, or ask for the next usable paragraph.
                    

                    

                        research
                        Let the assistant search the web and read your notes before it answers.
                    

                    

                        import
                        Evernote sync is included in the Mac app, so the old archive becomes writing context.
                    

                    

                        own
                        Free MIT-licensed software, source on GitHub, and a signed/notarized DMG you can download and run.
